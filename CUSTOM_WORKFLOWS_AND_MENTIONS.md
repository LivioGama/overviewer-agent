# Custom Workflows and Mentions

## Overview

This document outlines how custom workflows and custom @mentions can be utilized to enhance the overview-agent experience. By leveraging these features, clients can tailor the functionality of the overview-agent to better suit their specific needs.

## Custom Workflows

Custom workflows allow clients to define specific sequences of actions that the overview-agent can perform. These workflows can be triggered by various events or conditions, enabling a more automated and efficient process.

### Benefits
- **Automation**: Reduce manual intervention by automating repetitive tasks.
- **Customization**: Tailor workflows to meet specific business requirements.
- **Efficiency**: Streamline processes to save time and resources.

## Custom @Mentions

Custom @mentions provide a way to trigger specific functionalities within the overview-agent. By mentioning certain keywords or phrases, users can activate predefined actions or workflows.

### Benefits
- **Quick Access**: Easily trigger actions without navigating through complex menus.
- **Flexibility**: Define mentions that align with your team's terminology and processes.
- **Integration**: Seamlessly integrate with existing communication tools to enhance collaboration.

## Conclusion

Implementing custom workflows and mentions can significantly improve the functionality and user experience of the overview-agent. By providing these customization options, clients can ensure that the tool aligns closely with their operational needs and enhances overall productivity.
